Who We Are

We embarked on our journey with a vision: to deliver innovative modular furniture solutions of the highest quality, meeting the dynamic needs of the market. OMF represents a fusion of kitchen, living, and workspace modular furniture solutions. Our creations not only transform ordinary spaces into stunning dreamscapes but also streamline everyday tasks with unmatched ease, backed by our limitless manufacturing capacity.

Modular Innovations

The evolution of furniture design mirrors the progression of human civilization, encapsulating lifestyle, materials, and technological advancements of each era. At OMF, we consistently stay ahead of the curve, pioneering practical innovations and crafting distinctive designs that resonate with our clients. Our focus lies in creating kitchen solutions that not only prioritize functionality but also reflect the unique personalities of their owners.

  • Eco-friendly Materials
  • Smart Furniture Tech
  • Bold Textures
  • Biophilic Design

We stay at the forefront of the latest design trends in home and office spaces to create luxurious dream environments for our clients. With our materiO library, we cater to clients' unique needs using an extensive selection of decorative materials, including laminates, veneers, metals, glass, mesh, textiles, and acoustic solutions.
