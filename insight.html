Design + Build your story

Nestled in Surat, India's renowned silk city, our roots run deep in the furniture industry. Our state-of-the-art factory, a testament to modern design and creative craftsmanship, transforms dreams into tangible realities. Spanning over 50,000 square feet amidst lush landscapes, this is where the magic of production unfolds. Our experience centre, sprawling across 5,000 square feet in the same vibrant city, showcases live displays of diverse modular kitchens, living spaces, and workspaces solutions. From conception to fabrication to execution, our dynamic team comprises individuals from all walks of life—varying ages, genders, and backgrounds—coming together to create an inclusive environment where challenges are embraced, and dream ideas are brought to life.

Why OMF

Creativity, Trust, Flexibility and Personalisation are our driving forces.

We believe in the essence of every living space—a soul waiting to be unveiled. It's the passion, expertise, and creativity we pour into every design, manufacturing process, and application. Within it resides an internal universe, woven from the dreams, desires, feelings, and stories of those who inhabit it daily. We strive to interpret and transform these vital spaces—whether for living or working—into authentic reflections of their occupants. This convergence of affinities, shared experiences, and relationships shapes our vision of space. It transcends mere products, imbuing materials, technologies, and design with profound personal significance, crafting individual project stories that resonate deeply.
